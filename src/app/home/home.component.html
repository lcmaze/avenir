Two hours a day for 5 days a week (Mon-Fri) is the schedule for below mentioned courses. Add-on weekend batches are also available.

DAHTM

(Diploma in Aviation, Hospitality, Travel and Airport Management)
ELIGIBLITY CRITERIA : 17 - 24 year, for 12th pass students & above
DURATION : 1 Year

Aviation Management deals with the study of airlines, airports, and business associated with the aerospace industry. Customer handling, ticketing, international tourism board, travel and hospitality, and in-flight services are included in aviation management.

DHTCS

(Diploma in Hospitality, Travel and Customer Service)
ELIGIBLITY CRITERIA : 17 - 24 year, for 12th pass students & above
DURATION : 1 Year

Having a good connection with customers consistently encourages you to have a lovely and blissful workplace. Travel around the globe with your friendly customers.
